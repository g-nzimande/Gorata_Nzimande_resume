About Me

My name is Gorata, I was born in Ramotswa, a village about 40km north east of Gaborone, Botswana. I grew in Gaborone until the age of 9 and then spent most of my life in Johannesburg, South Africa, where I currently reside.

After high school I took a gap year to spend some time reflecting and deciding what it is I really wanted to do with my life going forward,I couldn't come to a solid decision so I got into the job market, working in various industries and gaining vast experience as I got older. I gained experience in junior roles and management roles where my duties ranged from customer service to project management. Two things that have been at the forefront of anything I do, are creativity and attention to detail.

I'm very passionate about marrying creativity and innovation in the way that I think about problem solving. My exposure to different working environments, leadership styles, learning paths and a diversity of people, leads to me constantly want to innovate and improve processes. As of 2020 I'm embarking on a journey to become a better equipped and skilled programmer through online self-study and bootcamps.

When I'm not in front of the computer, I'm exercising, learning more about reptiles, watching documentaries, immersed in DIY activities or just resting.
